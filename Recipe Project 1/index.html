Recipe for Majestic Shrimp Garlic and Butter Scampi

The Shrimp Scampi that is being curated in this recipe,
is one that has buttery edges of Shrimp, and has the fluorescent scent of heaven.
Think about the Garlic crackling in the pot, alongside the beautiful golden butter.


Garlic and butter in the air, ready to be made by you the chef.

Ingredients Required

  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 pounds of shrimp prawns
  • Salt and ground fresh black pepper
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1/4 of a cup of chopped parsley

Ingredients

  1. Heat the Olive Oil and put 2 tablespoons of butter in a large pan. Add garlic and saute the garlic from around 30 seconds to one minute. Then add seasoning to the Shrimp with salt and pepper then saute for around one to two minutes, then flip.
  2. Pour in white wine or some form of broth, and add crushed red pepper flakes if you choose to use it. Simmer the wine for about one to two minutes, until one half of the shrimp is fully cooked through.
  3. Stir the butter that is remaining as well as lemon juice and parsley.
  4. (Optional) Serve over rice of anykind, or pasta, garlic bread or steamed vegetables.
